Billing & refunds

Frequently asked questions

How do I get a refund?
Use the refund form above with the email and transaction ID from your receipt, or email us at profile-pro@supportapp.center. We review every request and reply, usually within 24 hours.
How long do refunds take?
Once approved, refunds are issued back to your original payment method and typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Email us with the address you used to subscribe and we'll cancel it and confirm. If you subscribed through the App Store or Google Play, you can also cancel in your device's subscription settings.
Is Profile Pro a scientific or medical service?
No. Profile Pro is for entertainment and self-discovery only. It does not perform medical, genetic, biometric or forensic analysis, and results are not definitive.
Do you store my photo?
No. Your image is processed to generate your profile and then deleted. We don't store, sell or share your photos.
