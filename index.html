Frequently asked questions

Is this scientific?
No. Profile Pro is made for entertainment and self-discovery only. It does not perform medical, genetic, biometric or forensic analysis, and results are not definitive claims about who you are.
Do you store my photo?
No. Your image is processed to generate your profile and then deleted. We don't store, sell or share your photos.
How accurate are the results?
Results are AI-generated interpretations designed to be fun and shareable — think of them as a playful mirror, not a factual report.
How much does it cost and how do I cancel?
You can start with a free preview. A Profile Pro membership unlocks the full report and can be cancelled anytime from your account settings. See our Terms for details.
